Top 4 Indonesian Bank Analysis

The dataset used for this analysis is obtained from Startup Campus's Mentor. The provided folder contains several datasets for each bank. Below is an explanation of each:

  • Balancesheet: Contains yearly company information such as Share Issued, Total Debt, Tangible Book Value, Net Tangible Assets, and more.
  • Cashflow: Contains yearly data on the company's cash flow.
  • Corp_actions: Contains information on the dates corporate actions were taken, including stock splits and dividend distributions.
  • Historical_value: Contains daily information on stock prices and trading volumes.
  • Income_stat: Contains information on the company's Net Income.
  • Key_stat: Contains current information on the company's valuation.

We decided to use 4 top banks in Indonesia: Bank A, Bank B, Bank C, Bank D

Our analysis consists of the following sections:

  • Business Understanding: Bussiness Problem, Goals, Solutioning.
  • Data Understanding: Data Information, Pattern Discovery.
  • Data Preprocessing: Data Cleaning, Transformasi Data, Dimentionality Reduction, Data Splitting.
  • EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis): Historical Value Graph, Descriptive Statistics.
  • Feature Engineering: Creation of four features that correlate with business goals.
  • Modeling: Baseline Model, Hyperparamter Tuning, Evaluation Metrics, One-year Forecasting.
  • Insight: Insights from the analysis of each bank.
  • Business Recomendation: Recommendations based on the analysis goals.

Top 4 Indonesian Bank Analysis Interactive Dashboard

Feature Selection

In the interactive dashboard, I used several features including:

Dimension

  • Date

    • Show historical data from 2014 until 2024, other feature from 2021 until 2023, and as a filter.

Metric

  • Close Stock Price

    • Shows each bank the closing stock price day by day from 2014 to 2024.

  • Open Stock Price

    • Shows each bank the opening stock price day by day from 2014 to 2024.

  • Stock Volume

    • Show each bank stock volume day by day from 2014 to 2024.

  • Stock Return

    • Show each bank return day by day from 2014 to 2024

  • Volatilty Value

    • Show each bank volatilty value per year from 2021 to 2023.

  • Current Ratio

    • Show each bank current ratio per year from 2021 to 2023.

  • Debt to Equity Ratio

    • Show each debt to equity per year from 2021 to 2023.

  • Net Profit Margin

    • Show each bank net profit margin per year from 2021 to 2023.
